About Us

Laat Saheb is a distinguished name in the ethnic fashion landscape, proudly carrying forward a legacy that dates back to 1965. Rooted in tradition yet inspired by contemporary style, the brand has consistently delivered finely crafted ethnic wear that reflects heritage, elegance, and timeless sophistication. With decades of expertise, Laat Saheb seamlessly blends premium quality fabrics, intricate craftsmanship, and modern design sensibilities to create collections that stand out. Committed to making luxury accessible, the brand offers refined, high-end fashion at an affordable range, ensuring that every customer experiences grandeur without compromise.
